What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox video game first made by Markus "Notch" Persson.
It was sold to Microsoft in 2014 for 2.5 billion American dollars.
Over 238 million copies of the game have been sold.
This makes it the best-selling video game of all time.

5 Fun Facts about Minecraft!
1. Creepers were originally supposed to be pigs!
2. Steve's model was taken from notch's game "Zombie Town".
3. The first version of Minecraft was developed in 6 days!
4. 100 days in minecraft translates to 33 hours in real life!
5. Minecraft's first version was called "cave game"
